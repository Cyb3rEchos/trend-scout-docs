Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect and process publicly available App Store data including app names, categories, rankings, prices, and ratings. This data is used solely to provide trend analysis and market insights.

Personal Information

Trend Scout does not collect personal information, user accounts, or tracking data. The app operates without requiring user registration or personal data submission.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Hosts our trend analysis database (EU-compliant)
  • Hugging Face AI: Powers our name generation feature (anonymized requests)
  • Apple App Store: Source of public app ranking data

Data Usage

All data collected is publicly available App Store information used to:

  • Generate market trend reports
  • Rank app opportunities by market potential
  • Provide AI-powered naming suggestions
  • Deliver competitive intelligence insights

Data Storage

Trend data is stored securely and updated daily. No personal user data is stored or transmitted.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All app data shown is publicly available information.

Children's Privacy

Trend Scout is designed for developers and entrepreneurs. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
